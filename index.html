What is Fantaland Token?

Fantaland token, is a hyper-deflationary token, built on the BSC mainnet, focused on being the native currency of the FANTALAND metaverse.

Being a cross chain launchpad token with a unique P2E game, and earnable NFTs, it aims to be used within the FANTALAND ecosystem to empower and incentivize a decentralized community building at the forefront of web3.
